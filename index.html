ESR Self Service Hub

ESR Self Service Hub

Welcome to the NHS Electronic Staff Record (ESR) system. ESR is an integrated workforce management solution developed by the NHS for the NHS. It's designed to support the entire employee lifecycle, from hiring to retirement, and is currently used by 99% of NHS Organisations.

The system allows you to interact with it anytime, from any device, at any location, empowering you to take ownership of your data.

Employee Self Service

ESR Employee Self-Service

Employee self-service (ESS) puts you in control of your employment records and can can be accessed anywhere from a PC, tablet or mobile device. You can also download the ESR app.
